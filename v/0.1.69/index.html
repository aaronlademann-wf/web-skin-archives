Introducing Wdesk

Need reasons to love Wdesk? Look no further.

By nerds, for nerds.

Inspired by the amazing work of @mdo and @fat and ported for WebFilings by @alademann, Wdesk utilizes Sassy CSS with Compass, to help the nerds at WebFilings do awesome stuff for their customers.

Made for everyone.

Wdesk has been built from the ground up to push WebFilings forward. It looks and behaves great on the latest smartphones, tablets, and desktops
(including support for IE8+).

Packed with features.

A -column responsive grid, dozens of components, JavaScript UI Plugins, typography, form controls, and much, much more.
